Information We Collect

This extension does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We have no servers, no databases, and no analytics.

Data Storage

  • Lottery results data is cached locally in your browser's chrome.storage.local for faster loading.
  • No data is ever sent to external servers other than fetching lottery results from the public API.
  • All cached data remains entirely on your device and under your control.
  • You can clear all cached data at any time by removing the extension.
